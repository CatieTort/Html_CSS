Fastest Roller Coasters

Formula Rossa

Formula Rossa is the fastest roller coaster in the world with the top speed of 150 mph. This roller coaster is located in Ferrari world of Abhu Dhabi. Formulla Rossa accelerates from zero to top speed of 150 miles per hour in just five seconds.

Kingda Ka

Kingda Ka at six flags great adventure park is the fastest roller coaster in the United States. The hydraulic launch mechanism in the ride helps it to gain top speed of 128 mph in just 3.5 seconds. This blazing speed also helps the ride to climb the 456 feet tall hill quickly.

Top Thrill Dragster

Top thrill dragster is the second fastest roller coaster in the United States situated in Ohio. It is the first ever strata coaster in the world, a complete circuit roller coaster that exceeds 400 ft in height. The ride will accelerate from 0 to 120 mph in just 4 seconds.

Dodonpa

Dodonpa is the fastest roller coaster in Japan located at Fuji-Q Highland amusement park in Yamanashi. The ride goes up to the speed of 107 mph in 1.8 seconds. Impressively, it is 2.7 times faster than the acceleration due to gravity. In that way, Dodonpa offers floating experience for its riders.

Superman: Escape from Krypton

Superman: escape from Krypton is a steel shuttle roller coaster at six flags magic mountain park in Valencia of California. The linear synchronous motors will accelerate this roller coaster from 0 to 100 mph in just 7 seconds. This roller coaster will rise to a height of 415 feet like Superman.

Steel Dragon 2000

Steel dragon 2000 is the longest and sixth fastest roller coaster in the world. It is also the second fastest roller coaster in Japan. Steel dragon 2000 rise up 318 feet and the first hill is at 307 feet. During the first drop, steel dragon 200 would accelerate up to a speed of 95 miles per hour.
